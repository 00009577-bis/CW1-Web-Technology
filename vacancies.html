Procedure of applying for a vacancy:

  1. Enter the page "Vacancies" and choose your favorite position.
  2. Enter the page "Vacancies" and choose your favorite position.
  3. If you meet all the requirements specified in the vacancy, we will contact you within 5 working days will and invite you for an interview. Depending on the complexity of the job, we might have 2 or 3 stages of the meeting with the candidates at various levels, including additional testing.
  4. Following the first meeting, we will notify you via e-mail notification if you pass to the next stage. If you meet our requirements, we will invite you to the next stage.
  5. At the end of the second phase we will also let you know whether you're going through the following steps. We reserve the right to reject your candidacy at all stages of an interview, in the case of lack of knowledge or skills. The decision about your employment will be made jointly, on the basis of the results obtained through the steps.
  6. All operating conditions are announced only at the last meeting. However, this does not prevent you to share with us your expectations from working with us on the first meeting.

Frequently Asked Questions

I have not found a suitable job, but I really want work with/for you!

If you did not find an interesting position for you, do not worry, go to our section of "Career" as often as possible, and we hope that very soon you will find a position that you are interested in.


I have sent the questionnaire, but did not receive confirmation that I have registered on the job.

If within 3 hours after filling in an online questionnaire, you do not get a notice, please contact us through the section "Ask a Question", indicate at the problem, and we will contact you by e-mail, or phone call .


How to get a job in Murad Buildings or its partners?

If you want your candidacy reviewed, you must complete an online questionnaire on your favorite position. If you simply leave your resume in our office, we cannot guarantee that you will be contacted by our experts.


Whether I be taken on the recommendation of my friends or acquaintances?

We welcome any channels through which you can learn about our vacancies. However, to ensure that your candidacy reviewed, you will need to go through the official registration on our site, and all stages of consideration, including the possibility of testing, group exercises and interviews. Your acquaintances, friends or relatives may not participate in the consideration of your candidacy. The decision about your employment will be made based on the collective decision of all employees who took part in the examination and test results.
